### 1. `rex_01_anatomy.png` — Candle anatomy
```
What is this candle telling you? 🕯️

Every candle tells a story:
• Body = open price → close price
• Wick (the tail) = the highest/lowest price it touched
• Green = price went up 🟢 | Red = price went down 🔴

Learn this and you can read ANY chart.
```

### 2. `rex_02_engulfing.png` — Engulfing
```
When a BIG candle completely "swallows" the small one before it… 😳

Control just changed hands:
• Big green swallows red → BUYERS win 🟢
• Big red swallows green → SELLERS win 🔴

Strongest when it shows up at support or resistance.

💬 Have you ever traded this pattern? Tell me in the comments!

#XAUUSD #gold #forex #engulfing #priceaction
```

### 3. `rex_03_pinbar.png` — Pin Bar
```
That long "tail" on a candle = a REJECTION 🚫

The longer the tail, the harder price was pushed back:
• Long tail below → buyers rejected lower prices → likely UP
• Long tail above → sellers rejected higher prices → likely DOWN

📌 Save it + send it to a friend who's just starting out!

#XAUUSD #gold #pinbar #priceaction #forex
```

### 9. `rex_09_sr.png` — Support & Resistance
```
The "floor" and the "ceiling" of price 🏠

• Resistance (ceiling) = where sellers keep stepping in → price struggles to rise
• Support (floor) = where buyers keep defending → price struggles to fall

Price bounces between them. Trade AT the level, not in no-man's-land.

📌 Save this as your compass every time you open a chart!

#XAUUSD #gold #supportresistance #priceaction #forex
```

### 10. `rex_10_trend.png` — Reading the trend
```
Trade WITH the trend, don't argue with it 📈📉

• Uptrend = each high higher, each low higher
• Downtrend = highs and lows both getting lower

Go with the trend and the odds are on your side. Fight it and you're just donating money.

🔁 Share this with a friend who loves "catching the bottom" against the trend!

#XAUUSD #gold #trend #trendtrading #forex
```
