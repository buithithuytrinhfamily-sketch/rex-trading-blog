Who’s behind the channel

I treat a trading account the way I ran my company: with books, rules, and risk limits.

For five years I signed the purchase orders, capped the costs, and audited the numbers every night. Then I handed three years of company profit to a “fixed returns, zero risk” promise — and one morning it was simply gone.

I tried to win it back fast and burned four accounts doing it. What finally worked wasn’t a strategy anyone sells you. It was a notebook: every trade planned before entry, journaled after exit, losers written down first. The account stopped dying.

This channel is that notebook out loud — XAUUSD setups and the reasons behind them. Watch how I handle the losses, not just the wins. That’s where the method actually lives.

— Rex
